Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior yoga experience?

Not at all! Our classes are designed to accommodate all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our instructors provide modifications and variations to ensure everyone can practice safely and effectively.

What should I bring to class?

We provide yoga mats, blocks, and props. Just bring yourself, wear comfortable clothing, and arrive with an open mind. We recommend bringing a water bottle and arriving 10-15 minutes early for your first class.

Can I try a class before committing to a membership?

Absolutely! We offer a complimentary first class for all new students. This gives you a chance to experience our teaching style, meet our instructors, and explore our studio before making any commitment.

Are online classes live or recorded?

We offer both! Live-streamed classes allow you to practice in real-time with our instructors and community. We also have a library of recorded classes available 24/7 for your convenience.
